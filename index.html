iOS 18 launched: 10 new features it is bringing to iPhone

June 11, 2024 by Steve

Apple launched the iOS 18 during WWDC last night. Here are 10 new features that will be coming to the iPhone after the launch of the all-new operating system.

Apple hosted its annual developer conference --WWDC--last night and the event was packed with various announcements aimed at improving users' overall experience. During the event, Apple gave us a sneak peek into iOS 18, their next big release packed with exciting new features. From a revamped Photos app to enhanced customisation options, Mail improvements, satellite messaging, and much more, iOS 18 brings an array of new features to your iPhone.

Customisation options

Talking about customisation first, iOS 18 introduced a new approach to customising the Home Screen, Lock Screen, and Control Center.

Photos app gets a redesign

The Photos app has received its most significant redesign ever, unifying photo libraries into a single view. With new collections, it will be easier to browse through your photos based on themes like recent days, favourite people and pets, and trips.

Top 15 coolest launches at the Apple WWDC 2024

June 11, 2024 by Mark

Apple's Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC) is underway, with a focus on AI for enhanced user experience across iPhone, iPad, Mac, and potentially other devices. Let's look at 15 of the coolest launches for this year's WWDC.

Apple has now, integrated ChatGPT across iOS 18, iPadOS 18, and the new macOS Sequoia. Siri can now access ChatGPT for detailed responses, while taking user consent for each interaction for data privacy. ChatGPT also aids in generating content and images through Apple's Writing Tools.

The latest version of Siri can now help users with improved language understanding, and maintaining context across interactions. Users can type or speak to Siri. Siri can now add addresses from messages to contacts and even find specific articles from a Reading List.
